Opportunities and Risks

Governments on social media

Opportunities

Opportunities - there are many opportunities that open up when governments start a social media account, such as cheaper communication costs between them and the citizens, also social media is used a lot during crisis situations as you can access social media with ease on your phone and almost everyone is on it these days which is why lockdowns and natural disasters are now being announced on social media. Social media also provides better feedback as with other forms of communicating their is no easy way for citizens to share their thoughts on current matters for the government to see but with social media they can post their thoughts up for everyone including the government to see which allows for more feedback which the government can then take in and make more informed decisions.

Risks

Risks involved - there are a few risks involved when it comes to this topic, one of them being impersonators, people or accounts posing as a government official or the government which can send out fake information to citizens, they will eventually get shut down but not before releasing a lot of false information and causing damage. Another risk is hacking, as a real government account can be hacked which then can be used to release false information but it will affect a lot more people as it is being posted on the real account.
